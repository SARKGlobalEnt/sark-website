Manufacturing Facility & Infrastructure

The manufacturing facility of SARK Cold/Screw Pressé is purpose-built to support hygienic operations, efficient workflow, and scalable edible oil production.

The infrastructure is designed to enable smooth coordination across processing, storage, and packaging functions, ​ ensuring controlled operations and consistent production output.

Facility Layout & Operations

SARK Cold/Screw Pressé operates from a dedicated edible oil manufacturing facility with a ​ purpose-planned layout that supports organized material movement and operational clarity.

Clearly defined zones for processing, storage, and packaging help maintain workflow discipline and support day-to-day manufacturing efficiency.

Machinery & Processing Infrastructure 

The facility is equipped with cold/screw press extraction systems supported by food-grade stainless steel oil collection and filtration infrastructure.

Core equipment is selected to enable stable performance and dependable output across regular production cycles, forming the backbone of the manufacturing operation.

Storage & Packaging Setup 

Extracted oil is transferred to stainless steel storage tanks before being routed to the filling and packaging line.

The packaging setup is designed to support multiple pack formats, allowing flexibility for retail and bulk distribution requirements.

Packaging configurations are planned to support current demand while allowing phased expansion as volumes grow.

Utilities & Support Systems

The facility is supported by essential utilities including reliable power supply, water systems for cleaning and maintenance, and appropriate ventilation and lighting for manufacturing operations.

These systems are integrated to support safe working conditions and uninterrupted production flow.

Operational Readiness & Scalability

SARK’s infrastructure is designed with scalability in mind, enabling the facility to support increasing demand while maintaining consistent operations across production batches.

The setup allows for planned capacity expansion and process optimization without disrupting ongoing operations.

Safety & Hygiene Practices

Food-grade materials are used across the infrastructure, with controlled handling and storage practices followed throughout the facility.

Routine cleaning, monitoring, and standard operating procedures help maintain hygienic conditions and ​support reliable manufacturing outcomes.

Our mission is to produce traditionally extracted edible oils with a focus on purity, hygiene, and consistent quality for everyday Indian cooking.
